About this space

A former 1920s cinema converted into a high-ceiling warehouse in the heart of Mitte, 400 metres from Rosa-Luxemburg-Platz. Originally built to host wedding receptions and film shoots, "The Wedding Hangar" now serves as a modern B2B storage facility with 24/7 access, two loading docks, an on-site forklift, and direct ring-road access. Ideal for production archives, pallet storage, seasonal overflow, and light assembly.
